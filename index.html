History & Evolution

Founded: 1893 by Caleb Bradham, a pharmacist in New Bern, North Carolina. Originally called "Brad's Drink," renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898.

Name Origin: Derived from "dyspepsia" (indigestion) and the kola nut, reflecting its original marketing as a digestive aid.
